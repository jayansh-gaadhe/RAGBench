ACME CORP EMPLOYEE HANDBOOK (2024 EDITION)

SECTION 1: LEAVE POLICY
Full-time employees accrue 20 days of paid annual leave per year. Annual leave accrues at a rate of 1.67 days per completed month of service. Unused annual leave of up to 5 days may be carried over into the next calendar year; any balance above 5 days is forfeited on December 31. Employees must request annual leave at least 7 days in advance through the HR portal, except in emergencies.

SECTION 2: SICK LEAVE
Employees are entitled to 10 days of paid sick leave per year. Sick leave does not carry over and resets on January 1. A medical certificate is required for any sick leave exceeding 3 consecutive days. Sick leave cannot be converted to cash and is not paid out upon termination.

SECTION 3: REMOTE WORK
Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week with manager approval. Fully remote arrangements require Director-level approval and are reviewed every 6 months. The company provides a one-time home-office stipend of $500 for fully remote employees. Remote employees must be reachable during core hours of 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM in their assigned time zone.

SECTION 4: EXPENSE REIMBURSEMENT
Business expenses must be submitted within 30 days of being incurred. Expenses above $200 require pre-approval from a manager. Meal expenses during business travel are capped at $75 per day. Receipts are mandatory for all reimbursements regardless of amount. Reimbursements are processed in the payroll cycle following approval.

SECTION 5: PARENTAL LEAVE
Primary caregivers are entitled to 16 weeks of paid parental leave. Secondary caregivers are entitled to 6 weeks of paid parental leave. Parental leave must be taken within 12 months of the birth or adoption of a child. Employees must give at least 8 weeks notice before commencing parental leave.

SECTION 6: PERFORMANCE REVIEWS
Performance reviews are conducted twice per year, in June and December. Ratings range from 1 (below expectations) to 5 (exceptional). Employees rated 4 or above are eligible for the annual bonus pool. A rating of 1 triggers a 60-day performance improvement plan (PIP).

SECTION 7: EQUIPMENT POLICY
Each employee is issued a company laptop. Laptops must be returned within 5 business days of termination. The company is not liable for personal data stored on company devices. Lost or stolen equipment must be reported to IT within 24 hours.
